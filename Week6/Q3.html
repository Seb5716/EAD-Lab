About CBIT
Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technology (CBIT) is an autonomous engineering college located in Gandipet, Hyderabad, Telangana. Established in 1979, it is affiliated to Osmania University and offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering and Technology.
